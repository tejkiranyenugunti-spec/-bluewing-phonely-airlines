# BlueWing Phonely Airlines — Refund and Change Policies

> This is the document uploaded to Phonely's Knowledge Base. The agent retrieves from it via RAG when callers ask policy questions like *"what is your refund policy?"* or *"can I change my flight?"*.

---

## Refund Policy

### 24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation

All tickets purchased directly through BlueWing Phonely Airlines may be cancelled within 24 hours of booking for a full refund, provided the booking was made at least 7 days before departure. No cancellation fee applies during this window.

### Refundable Fares

Refundable fares may be cancelled at any time before departure for a full refund to the original payment method. Refunds are typically processed within 7 to 10 business days.

### Non-Refundable Fares

Non-refundable fares are not eligible for cash refunds after the 24-hour window. However, the value of the ticket, minus a 75 dollar service fee, will be issued as a travel credit valid for 12 months from the original booking date.

---

## Change Policy

### Free Changes Within 24 Hours

You may change your travel date, time, or route free of charge within 24 hours of booking, provided the change is made at least 7 days before departure.

### Standard Change Fees

Outside the 24-hour window, the following change fees apply per passenger per booking:

- Same-day flight change: **$50**
- Date change to a different day: **$100**
- Route change to a different city: **$150**

Any fare difference between the original and new flight is additional.

### Same-Day Standby

For a flat fee of **$25**, passengers may request standby on an earlier or later flight on the same day of travel, subject to seat availability.

---

## Baggage Policy

### Carry-On

Each passenger may bring one carry-on bag up to 22 × 14 × 9 inches and one personal item free of charge.

### Checked Baggage

- First checked bag: **$35**
- Second checked bag: **$45**
- Bags must weigh under 50 pounds

---

## Customer Support

Customer support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Callers can be transferred to a live agent at any point during this call by simply asking to speak to a human or to customer support.

---

## Contact

For booking inquiries, you may call BlueWing Phonely Airlines anytime. Confirmation numbers and itineraries are sent via SMS for US phone numbers and email for international contacts.
